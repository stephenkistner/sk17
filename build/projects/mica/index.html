The Challenge

What does business growth mean in a changing world?

Imagine a hot summer day in the city. A thick soup of stagnant air surrounds your body as you step outside. How does it feel? Pretty freaking uncomfortable.

Now imagine a snowy December evening. It's picturesque, but the moment you step outside the cold is harsh and cutting.

I wanted to evoke these same feelings through dynamic visual compositions.

How it feels outside also hinges upon more than just temperature. A summer day with a breeze feels drastically different than one with no breeze and high humidity. I needed to develop a system that took all of these conditions into account.
