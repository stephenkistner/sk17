The Challenge

What does “business growth” mean?

The ad campaign was part of a larger initiative developed by the Carbone Smolan Agency for BCG called Onwards & Upwards. Leveraging content from their TED@BCG video series, Onwards & Upwards championed BCG's perspective on fostering growth in a rapidly changing world.

The project consisted of a microsite featuring original content by BCG and the video ad campaign. CSA brought me on to develop the post-roll video ad that would air on TED.com.

Onwards & Upwards had a sister project called Ahead of the Curve, produced a year earlier. Like it's accompanying ad, this one needed to be 1.5 minutes long, and would air after the primary content on TED.com.

The video had to:

  1. Feel similar to its sister video
  2. Use the existing design elements
  3. End on the Onwards & Upwards title lockup
But what is business growth?

Admittedly, I had never heard of BCG before and, after being briefed, only had a vague understanding of what they did. While our copywriter Brittney worked on the first drafts of the script, I examined some of BCG's videos and written content to better understand the vision of the project.

The Approach

An essential force of nature

BCG's writings covered a diverse range of topics from pharmaceuticals to the booming tech industry. The one connection they all had was the innate nature of growth.
